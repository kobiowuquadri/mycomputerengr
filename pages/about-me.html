About
myComputerENGR

myComputerENGR is Lagos’ dedicated B2B device repair company for businesses and enterprises. Since our establishment, we have helped thousands of individuals, entrepreneurs, small businesses, and large organisations get their devices back in perfect working condition.

Engineering Reliable Technology Solutions Across Nigeria.

What We Do

We repair, upgrade, and supply technology solutions for personal users and organizations across Nigeria. Whether you need an emergency smartphone screen replacement, a MacBook motherboard fix, or bulk PC servicing for your company, we handle it with professionalism.

Who We Are

Our team consists of certified technicians with deep expertise in Apple devices, PCs, and smartphones. We're authorised Apple support technicians, which means we use genuine parts and follow Apple's service standards while staying updated on the latest repair techniques.

Authorised Support

As authorised Apple support technicians, we have exclusive access to authentic components, specialized technical diagnostic frameworks, and original system pathways. This guarantees your premium hardware retains its original corporate warranty and structural performance.

Meet the Founder

Adetunji Adedayo is the founder/Lead Technician at myComputerENGR, serving individuals, businesses, and enterprises.

With a background spanning IT management, cybersecurity governance, and enterprise technology partnerships, Adedayo has overseen large-scale device deployment projects, built IT infrastructure and disaster recovery frameworks aligned with international standards, and led teams supporting startups, education centres, and corporate clients.

He holds certifications, including Apple Certified Mac Technician (ACMT),  ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Lead Implementer, Certified Cloud Security Knowledge (CCSK) and Certified Information Security Professional (CISSP), and combines hands-on technical expertise with a client-first approach to keep businesses running smoothly.
